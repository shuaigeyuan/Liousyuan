About Diep.io

Diep.io is an exciting multiplayer tank battle game where you control a tank in an arena filled with shapes and other players. Destroy shapes to gain experience points and level up your tank, unlocking new abilities and upgrades along the way.

The game features a variety of tank classes, each with unique strengths and weaknesses, allowing for diverse gameplay strategies. Whether you prefer aggressive combat or supportive roles, there's a tank class for every playstyle.

How to Play

Controls

  • Move your tank using the WASD keys or arrow keys
  • Aim and shoot with your mouse
  • Press Space to use special abilities (for certain tanks)
  • Press E to auto-fire
  • Press C to enable auto-spin

Tips for Success

  • Start by destroying shapes to level up quickly
  • Experiment with different tank upgrades to find your playstyle
  • Stay away from stronger players until you've upgraded
  • Balance your stat points across health, damage, and speed
  • Team up with other players in team modes for better survival

Game Modes

Free For All

Every player for themselves in an open arena. Destroy shapes and other players to become the strongest tank.

Team Deathmatch

Join one of several teams and work together to dominate the arena. Protect your teammates and coordinate attacks.

Domination

Capture and hold dominator turrets to win. Teams must work strategically to control these powerful positions.

Tank Classes

Twin

Fires two bullets simultaneously, providing increased firepower.

Sniper

Long-range specialist with powerful shots but slower reload speed.

Machine Gun

Rapid-fire tank with less accurate but numerous bullets.

Flank Guard

Shoots from both front and back for better defense.
