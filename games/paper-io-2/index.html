About Paper.io 2

Paper.io 2 is an exciting multiplayer territory conquest game where your goal is to claim as much of the map as possible. Control your block, draw boundaries to claim new territory, and try to eliminate other players by cutting across their trails before they complete their territory claim.

With its simple yet addictive gameplay, colorful visuals, and strategic depth, Paper.io 2 offers endless hours of fun as you compete to dominate the map. The game features smooth controls, various skins to unlock, and a competitive leaderboard to showcase your territorial dominance.

How to Play

Controls

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move your block
  • Move outside your territory to create a trail
  • Return to your territory to claim the area you've encircled
  • Avoid having your trail cut by other players

Tips for Success

  • Start with small territory claims to minimize risk
  • Watch other players' movements and plan your expansion accordingly
  • Try to cut off other players' trails when they're far from their territory
  • Use quick, short movements when surrounded by aggressive players
  • Aim for larger territory claims when fewer players are nearby

Global Leaderboard

Smooth Gameplay

Enjoy responsive controls and smooth animation that make territory conquest both satisfying and strategic.

Advanced Strategies

The Spiral Technique

Create a spiral pattern when claiming territory to maximize the area while minimizing the length of your vulnerable trail. This makes it harder for opponents to cut you off.

Border Hugging

Stay close to the edges of your territory when expanding. This reduces the length of your trail and makes it easier to retreat to safety if threatened.

Aggressive Expansion

When you notice a player with a large territory, try to cut through their area with quick, decisive moves. If successful, you can claim a significant portion of their territory in one go.

Defensive Play

If you already have a substantial territory, focus on defending it rather than expanding further. Make small, quick excursions and return to safety to avoid risking your position.
