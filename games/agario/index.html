Agar.io

Grow, consume, and dominate in this addictive multiplayer cell survival game.

About Agar.io

Agar.io is an exciting multiplayer game where you control a cell in a petri dish filled with other player-controlled cells and AI cells. Your objective is simple yet challenging: consume smaller cells to grow larger while avoiding being consumed by larger cells.

The game's minimalist design belies its deeply strategic gameplay. As you grow larger, you move more slowly, making you a bigger target for other players. You'll need to balance aggression with caution to survive and climb the leaderboard.

How to Play

Controls

  • Move your cell using your mouse or trackpad
  • Press Space to split your cell (when large enough)
  • Press W to eject mass (tactical move to feed allies or bait enemies)
  • Double-click to activate turbo mode (in certain game modes)

Tips for Success

  • Start by consuming small static cells to grow quickly
  • Hide near viruses (green spiky cells) to escape from larger predators
  • Use the split move to catch fleeing smaller cells
  • Team up with other players in team modes for better survival
  • Be patient - sometimes waiting for the right moment is better than constant aggression

Game Modes

Free For All

The classic mode where every player competes individually. Grow as large as possible and dominate the leaderboard.

Teams

Join one of several colored teams and work together to dominate the arena. Help teammates and coordinate attacks against rival teams.

Experimental

Try out new features and gameplay mechanics that aren't in the main game yet. Experience Agar.io in new and exciting ways.

Advanced Strategies

Virus Manipulation

Viruses can be your best friend or worst enemy. When you're small, hide behind them for protection. When you're large, feed viruses with mass (W key) to make them split larger opponents who are chasing you.

Split Tactics

Master the art of splitting. A well-timed split can help you catch prey, but splitting too much can leave you vulnerable. Remember that your cells will merge back together after a certain time.

Baiting

Eject a small amount of mass to bait smaller players into a false sense of security, then quickly move to consume them when they approach.

Corner Camping

The corners of the map often have fewer players, making them good places to grow safely in the early game. Just be careful not to get trapped by larger cells!
