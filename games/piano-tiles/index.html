About Piano Tiles

Piano Tiles is an addictive rhythm-based game that challenges your reflexes and timing. The concept is simple yet engaging: tap the black tiles as they scroll down the screen while avoiding the white tiles. Each successful tap produces a musical note, creating a melody as you play.

Originally released as a mobile game, Piano Tiles quickly became a worldwide sensation due to its perfect blend of simple gameplay and musical satisfaction. This online version brings the same exciting experience to your browser, allowing you to enjoy the game on any device.

How to Play

Basic Rules

  • Tap only the black tiles as they appear
  • Never tap the white tiles or you'll lose
  • Don't miss any black tiles or you'll lose
  • Try to keep up as the speed increases

Tips for Success

  • Focus on the bottom of the screen for upcoming tiles
  • Establish a rhythm with the music to improve timing
  • Use multiple fingers for faster sections
  • Practice regularly to improve your reflexes
  • Try to beat your high score with each attempt

Game Features

Each tap creates a musical note, allowing you to play beautiful piano melodies as you progress through the game. The satisfaction of creating music adds an extra layer of enjoyment.

Progressive Difficulty

The game gradually increases in speed as you play, providing a continuously challenging experience that tests your reflexes and concentration as you improve.
